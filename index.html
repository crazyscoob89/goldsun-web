Our Story
About Us

Some lessons can only be learned by watching someone you love pour their whole heart into their work.

Growing up, Elaine watched her grandmother — Abuela Rosa — build something from nothing. She remembers walking into that office, the New York skyline mirror on the wall, her grandmother on the phone, commanding every room she walked into. She remembers thinking: "I want to be a boss just like her." That image never left.

Decades later, that little girl grew into a woman who carries those lessons in everything she does — in the way she shows up for her clients, the way she handles the details, and the way she refuses to let anything fall through the cracks. Over the course of her career in the pool construction industry, Elaine has held positions across permit coordination, service management, scheduling, and operations — building a deep, hands-on knowledge of every facet of the industry. Her grandmother gave her the drive. The industry gave her the expertise. Gold Sun Permits brings them together.

We are a family-owned permit expediting service based in Casselberry, Florida. We specialize in residential and commercial pool permit coordination — because we believe every project deserves to start on solid ground, handled by people who treat it like their own.
